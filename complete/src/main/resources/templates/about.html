American Red Cross American Heart Association United Way

The American Red Cross is one of the largest nonprofit entities that provides disaster relief and military support. Their blood bank saves thousands of lives every year. The organization provides food, water, and shelter for people affected by fires and natural disasters. The American Red Cross helps military families cope with deployment. This includes delivering emergency communications and providing financial assistance when needed. Many veterans depend on the American Red Cross for emergency needs such as food and shelter as well as counseling referrals. The American Red Cross can also help veterans apply for benefits.

According to Forbes, United Way raised $3.71 billion dollars in 2015. United Way provides people with the resources they need to lead productive lives. Many families depend on the United Way for career training, job-search assistance, and credit counseling. The company provides a variety of health services including nutrition education, physical fitness programs, and professional medical care.
